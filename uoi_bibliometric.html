University of Idaho Press
Bibliometric Analysis

1966 – 2012 · subject headings · technical details · themes over time

Visualizations to help inform University of Idaho Press decisions on selecting legacy titles for republication. Original export data was standardized and deduplicated. Technical details were separated and page count data combined. See the button on the bottom of the interface to view raw data.

Publications Over Time

Each node is one title. Horizontal position = publication year. Vertical position = total page count. Scale represents subject heading count. Color represents genre. Hover for technical details. Select any node to open its library record.

Themes Over Time

Subject headings standardized into eight themes. Each bar group represents one decade.

Subject Frequency

Subject headings with three or more subgroups, arranged most-to-least frequent from top-left to bottom-right. Each heading ring has a unique color shared by its subgroup dots. Hover any ring for all titles under that heading; hover any dot to reveal the subgroup name and its titles.

Technical Details

All 125 titles with recorded page counts, sorted page count lowest to highest. Bars segmented by pages, dimensions, and illustration type count. Titles with no illustrations show only pages and dimensions. Click any bar to open its library record.
